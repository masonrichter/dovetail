Meet Our Team

Expert professionals dedicated to your financial success

Alan Myers

Principal

Alan brings over 30 years of experience in securities and wealth management, including positions at Morgan Stanley, Deutsche Bank, Credit Suisse, and Wells Fargo. In 2019, he founded his own RIA with Trails Edge Wealth Advisers, operating in a fiduciary capacity.

With a finance degree from the University of Wisconsin (1992), Alan combines academic excellence with practical expertise. He resides in Northbrook with his wife Shelley and their three children: Evan, Eli, and Alexis.

Glenn Farkas

Principal

As the owner of Avista Wealth Management since 2009, Glenn has established himself as a leader in investment advisory services. His 20+ year career spans both private wealth management and institutional equity research at firms including HOLT Value Associates, Credit Suisse, and Orion Capital Management.

Glenn holds a Masters in Finance from DePaul University and a BBA in Finance and Marketing from the University of Cincinnati, where he was a three-year letterman quarterback. He lives with his wife Pauline and their two college-aged children.

Joanna Jelenski

Operations Manager

With over 15 years of experience in private wealth management, Joanna has served in key operational roles at Morgan Stanley, Credit Suisse, and Wells Fargo advisors. Her expertise ensures smooth, efficient operations for our clients and team.

Joanna holds a degree in Criminal Justice from Northwestern Business College and resides in Chicago with her husband Mayday and their four children: Izabella, Maya, Yasmine, and Jacob.

Leo Levinson

Financial Analyst

A recent graduate of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Leo brings fresh perspectives and modern analytical approaches to our team. His studies in personal financial planning, combined with his successful completion of the CFP certification exam, demonstrate his commitment to excellence in wealth management.

Currently working towards his CFP designation, Leo represents the next generation of financial planning professionals.
